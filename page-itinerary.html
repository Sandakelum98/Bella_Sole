Day 01 - Arrival / Negombo

Upon arriving in Sri Lanka meet and assist at the airport by our
representative and transfer to Negombo from the airport.

Overnight stay at Hotel in Negombo.

Day 02 - Negombo / Pinnawala / Dambulla

In the morning, before you leave the coast behind, weave through stalls piled with shark, squid, barracuda and all types of sea creatures as the sellers call out their wares in Negombo fish market.

Drive inland to Dambulla and the “dry zone” in the heart of Sri Lanka’s cultural triangle.

En route visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage .

After that we will continue your journey to Dambulla. Check in to the hotel in Dambulla. Rest of the day is free to relax by the pool.

Overnight stay at Hotel in Dambulla.

Day 03 - Sigiriya / Minneriya / Dambulla

After Breakfast visit to Sigiriya .

After decent you can enjoy a cool drink of king coconut thambili.

In the evening get ready to do the Safari game in Minneriya National Park and Photograph the Gathering of Elephants.

Overnight stay at Hotel in Dambulla.

Day 04 - Dambulla / Village Tour / Polonnaruwa

Very early this morning you make the one and a half hours drive to Parakrama Lake.

We now take a bullock cart (Ox cart) which will transport us through the next part of the journey through dense shrub forests, home to many forest birds and other wildlife including monkeys, bear, python and even elephant  and also on the way you can experience the boat and Elephant ride.

After that we will continue your journey to a to the ancient city of Polonnaruwa , which reached its height of glory in the 12th century.

Overnight stay at Hotel in Polonnaruwa.

Day 05 - Polonnaruwa / Matale / Kandy

Our destination today is Kandy , Sri Lanka’s hill capital.

En route visit a Spice Garden in Matale… you can enjoy the activities in Spice Garden.

After that proceed to Kandy .

Check in to the Hotel. we head to Sri Lanka’s hill capital - the small lakeside city of Kandy.

Overnight stay at Hotel in Kandy.
